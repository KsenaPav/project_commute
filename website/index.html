About This Visualization

Who is it for and what is it about? This visualization helps students who prefer cycling to Moulon Campus find accommodation with the best commute times. It highlights student housing places.

Why does it matter? Choosing housing with a short bike commute saves time, improves daily comfort, and makes cycling a more enjoyable option.

How do you read it? You can zoom in and out using the buttons or the mouse wheel, and drag the map to explore different areas. The map is responsive and adjusts to the size of the container. By clicking on a student housing of interest you can see additional information about its address and rent price range.
